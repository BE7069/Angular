The art party theme is open for all - boys and girls - of any age.

Every participant will get an easle with a canvas. Canvasses are pre-printed with a picture.
Each kid will be able to choose the picture he/she would like to paint. Acrylic paints will be
provided along with brushes and a bottle with water. A quick lesson will be given as to how to
paint and blending colours.

The colours used are up to the participant. At the end of the day the finished product can
be taken home. Although each kid will be provided with an apron for the duration of the party
it is recommended that they wear older clothes as paint could be messed on them.

PriceL R140 per person
